Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data Skar collects, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, username, email address or phone number, city, occupation, and age. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile photo from Google.

Sleep Data

We collect your clock-in time, wake-up time, sleep duration, lock score, and morning ritual responses. If you connect Google Health Connect, we read sleep and health metrics with your explicit permission.

Usage Data

We track which features you use, your streak history, badge progress, city journey progress, and genre preferences. This helps us personalise your experience.

App Blocking Data

When you enable app blocking, Skar uses Android Accessibility Services to detect when blocked apps are opened during lockdown. We do not read the content of any other app. We only record whether a blocked app was opened to calculate your lock score.

Community Data

Confessions you post, friend connections, and paired lockdown data are stored to provide social features. Confessions are displayed anonymously but are linked to your account for moderation.

Device Information

We may collect device type, OS version, and app version for crash reporting and performance monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide and personalise the Skar experience, including sleep tracking, score calculation, streak tracking, city journey progress, leaderboard rankings, chronotype analysis, genre-specific content, and push notifications.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) with encryption at rest and in transit. Access to your data is protected by Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules that restrict access to your own account.

4. Data Sharing

We share your data only in the following cases: with other Skar users as part of social features you opt into (leaderboard, squad, paired lockdown), with service providers who help us operate the App (Firebase, analytics), and when required by law.

You can make your profile private in Settings to hide from the leaderboard.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access your data, correct inaccuracies, delete your account and all associated data, export your sleep data, opt out of leaderboards, and withdraw consent for health data access at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@skar.app or use the in-app settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all data is permanently removed within 30 days. Anonymous aggregated data may be retained indefinitely.

7. Children's Privacy

Skar is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Third-Party Services

Skar uses Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage), Google Health Connect (optional health data sync), and Google Play Billing (subscription payments). Each service has its own privacy policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@skar.app
